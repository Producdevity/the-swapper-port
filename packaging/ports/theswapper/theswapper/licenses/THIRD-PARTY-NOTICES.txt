The Swapper PortMaster package includes native compatibility shims and
third-party support libraries. The retail game files are not included.

Compatibility shims built from this repository:
- libs.aarch64/libfmodex.so
- libs.aarch64/libsteam_api.so
- libs.aarch64/libsdkencryptedappticket.so
- libs.aarch64/libwindows_stub.so
- libs.aarch64/kernel32
- libs.aarch64/dwmapi
- libs.aarch64/libkernel32.so
- libs.aarch64/libdwmapi.so

License: LICENSE.Port.txt
